    еҫ®з§ҜеҲҶз”ІдёӢеӨҚд№ (2)

    $$ 1.з”ұж–№зЁӢxyz+\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}=\sqrt{2},зЎ®е®ҡz=z(x,y),жұӮ\mathrm{d}z_{|(1,0,-1)} $$

    $$ еҜ№xжұӮеҒҸеҜј:yz+xy\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}+\frac{2x+2z\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}}{2\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}}=0 $$

    $$ еҜ№yжұӮеҒҸеҜјпјҡxz+xy\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}+\frac{2y+2z\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}}{2\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}}=0 $$

    д»Је…Ҙеҫ—пјҡ $$ \frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}{|(1,0,-1)}=1,\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}{|(1,0,-1)}=-\sqrt{2} $$

    $$ \therefore~~~~\mathrm{d}z=\mathrm{d}x-\sqrt{2}\mathrm{d}y $$

    $$ 2.и®ҫF(x-y,y-z,z-x)=0,Fе…·жңүиҝһз»ӯзҡ„еҒҸеҜјж•°пјҢдё”F_2'-F_3'\neq $$

    $$ 0,жұӮиҜҒ:\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}+\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}=0 $$

    $$ еҜ№xжұӮеҒҸеҜјпјҡF_1'-F_2'\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}+F_3'(\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}-1)=0~~~\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}=\frac{F_1'-F_3'}{F_2'-F_3'} \ еҜ№yжұӮеҒҸеҜјпјҡ-F_1'+F_2'(1-\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y})+F_3'\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}=0~~~\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}=\frac{F_2'-F_1'}{F_2'-F_3'} \дәҺжҳҜпјҢ\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}x}+\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}y}=1 $$

    $$ 3.и®Ўз®—\int_{\frac{1}{4}}^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}y\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{\sqrt{y}}e^{\frac{y}{x}}\mathrm{d}x+\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{1}\mathrm{d}y\int_{y}^{\sqrt{y}}e^{\frac{y}{x}}\mathrm{d}x $$

    иҪ¬еҢ–дёәе…ҲеҜ№xзҡ„з§ҜеҲҶпјҡ $$ еҺҹејҸ=\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{1}\mathrm{d}x\int_{x^2}^{x}e^{\frac{y}{x}}\mathrm{d}y=\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{1}(xe^{\frac{y}{x}})|_{x^2}^{x}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{3}{8}e-\frac{1}{2}e^{\frac{1}{2}} $$

    $$ 4.жұӮ\iint_{D}(\sqrt{x^2+y^2}+y)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\ D:x^2+y^2\le4~~(x+1)^2+y^2\ge1 $$

    $$ йҰ–е…Ҳ\iint_{D}y\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y=0 $$

    $$ еҺҹејҸ=\iint_{D+D1}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y-\iint_{D1}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\=\int_{0}^{2\pi}\mathrm{d}\theta\int_{0}^{2}r^2\mathrm{d}r-\int_{\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{3\pi}{2}}\mathrm{d}\theta\int_{0}^{-2cos\theta}r^2\mathrm{d}r=\frac{16\pi}{3}-\frac{32}{9} $$

    5. $$ и®Ўз®—дәҢйҮҚз§ҜеҲҶ\iint_{D}|x^2+y^2-1|\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y,е…¶дёӯDпјҡ(x,y)|0\le x\le1,0\le y\le1 $$

    $$ D_1,D_2\in D,D1:x^2+y^2\le1,D_2:x^2+y^2\ge1\ I=-\iint_{D1}x^2+y^2-1\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y+\iint_{D2}x^2+y^2-1\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\= \iint_{D}x^2+y^2-1\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y-2\iint_{D1}x^2+y^2-1\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\=-\frac{1}{3}+\frac{\pi}{4} $$
